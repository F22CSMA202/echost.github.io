Echo Theohar

Roleplay Americana

2018


Series of plastic and textile machine drawings printed with algorithmically generated phrases using Processing 3. Phrases are exported then vectorized, and printed with sharpie on a modified vinyl cutter arm.

Different iterations follow separate rulesets, such as the THANK YOU bag series consisting of the format random(verb) + random(pronoun). Each print is uniquely iterative in its semantics.
