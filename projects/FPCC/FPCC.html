Echo Theohar

Feminist Pornographic Collective Consciousness

2017


The feminist pornographic collective consciousness is defined by the constant battles between being a subject and an object of desire. That is not to say that subject and object must always exist as binary, however we recognize that in order to bring the mind-body issue closer to one another we must expand our notion and practice of love. For every kind of love defined by western mainstream media are understood in the framework of capitalism and patriarchy and exclusionary towards brown and sick bodies.

Toured Nah Fair 2017 and The LAABF 2017



In collaboration with a.m. darke, Yuehao Jiang, Sanglim Han, Ke Peng, Xin Xin, Mary Maggic, Julie Weitz, Hillary Cleary, and Alice Jung
